The growth of basketball culture in Durban in recent times coupled by the limited activities for the youth around Durban gave birth to the King of the Court (KOTC) tournament. The first tournament was in 2020 and progressively, the tournaments have helped the youth showcase and build their skills as well as encourage them to engage with other basketball players; thereby helping enhance their networking skills. The tournament has also encouraged diversity and welcomes players from all backgrounds.

Partnered with Nike Basketball, Spalding, and local community centers.
